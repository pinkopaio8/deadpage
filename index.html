An Anime Pic a Day

This app shows you an anime pic a day with a interesting quote below them. The app's goal is to let people think about their life, their family and their loved one, but with the pleasuring addition of an anime girl who looks into your eyes.

You can download this app in APK Format, or directly from the Play Store. The app will istantly tune with the time of your smartphone, and when it's midnight, the pic will change to a different one, and with a different quote.

I do not own any of the picture in this app. Every image you see has its own source at the top-left corner and almost every quote comes from the website brainyquote.com. Make sure to give them a visit. App made by Hakase & Naquiem
